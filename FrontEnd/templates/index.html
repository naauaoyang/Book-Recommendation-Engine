Introduction To Our Project

In our project, we build a recommendation system based on Mahout and Spark realizing three kinds of recommendation. The first one is recommending new books to to customers. This includes recommending books to the existing users based on their tastes and recommending reading list to new users based on booksâ€™ popularity. The second one is recommending friends to users based on their similarity. The third one is recommending potential useful reviews to users. Finally we use System G and build a Flask web application to visualize our findings.

This website is designed based on python Flask and the engine can recommend books and similar users for existing Amazon users and new users.

How to Recommend

Click "Sign In" in the menubar, and then enter your Amazon user Id. Click "Start!" Button. You will get your special Amazon recommendation.
